Me-MG: The Device to Connect you to your Physio!

This device is a small passive device which will sit on your knee to measure your muscle activation during your exercises. During your exercises the device will give you realtime feedback on your muscle activation, extension, and number of repetitions: giving you the confidence that you are performing the exercises correctly and are doing the best you can to recover quickly.

Once the device has been set up you and your physio can see your progress at any time on our dashboard simply by logging in! From there your physio can set goals for number of workouts and repetitions.

Realtime Feedback

After the device has been properly calibrated by your physiotherapist, whenever you use the device you will get instant feedback on your technique through the two on board LEDs, they will move from blue to green as you go from at rest to at the peak intensity of the exercise! Your aim should be to hit the green area with every repetition of the exercise, this means you have activated the correct muscle and have extended the region properly!

Extensive Dashboard

Once your physio as set up the device you and your physio will be able to log in and see all of the sessions which you have logged using the device! This gives your physio all the data they need to properly assess your rehabilitation, and assign you goals to get better faster! With the dashboard you can see a second by second recount of your workout showing how well you did every repetition, and whether you need to use more weight, or correct your technique!

Constant Connection

Since exercises are not always done in the comfort of your own home, we have made sure that the Me-MG can be used nearly anywhere within Australia! So there is no need for you to connect it to your phone, or Wi-Fi at home, just press the on button and away you go!
